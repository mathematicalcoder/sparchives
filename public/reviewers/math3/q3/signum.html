Signum Function

Here we explore the signum function, which is another major example of a piecewise-defined function.

The signum or sign function outputs the sign of the number as $-1$, $0$, or $1$. Specifically, $$\operatorname{sgn}(x)=\begin{cases}-1,&x\lt0\\0,&x=0\\1,&x\gt0\end{cases}$$
